Why Plastic Pollution is Dangerous

Plastic pollution is one of the most pressing environmental issues of our time. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste enter our oceans, harming marine life and ecosystems. Plastic doesn't biodegrade; it breaks down into microplastics that contaminate our food chain and water sources.
